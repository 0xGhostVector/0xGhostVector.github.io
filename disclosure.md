<div class="cve-content">

<p><em>Last updated: April 2026</em></p>

<h2>My Commitment</h2>
<p>I conduct security research ethically and responsibly. All vulnerabilities I discover are reported privately to the affected organisation before any public disclosure. I do not exploit vulnerabilities beyond what is necessary to demonstrate the issue.</p>

<h2>Disclosure Timeline</h2>
<p>I follow a standard coordinated disclosure timeline:</p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Day 0</strong> — Vulnerability discovered and verified in authorised scope</li>
  <li><strong>Day 1</strong> — Private report submitted to vendor/organisation</li>
  <li><strong>Day 7</strong> — Acknowledgement expected from vendor</li>
  <li><strong>Day 90</strong> — Standard disclosure deadline (industry standard)</li>
  <li><strong>Day 90+</strong> — Public disclosure regardless of patch status, with appropriate CVE</li>
</ul>

<p>Extensions beyond 90 days may be granted where:</p>
<ul>
  <li>The vendor is actively working on a fix and demonstrates progress</li>
  <li>The vulnerability affects critical infrastructure where immediate disclosure poses significant risk</li>
  <li>A formal extension is agreed in writing</li>
</ul>

<h2>Scope of My Research</h2>
<p>I only conduct security research on:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Systems I own or control</li>
  <li>Systems within the explicit scope of a bug bounty programme</li>
  <li>Systems where I have written authorisation from the owner</li>
</ul>

<h2>What I Will Not Do</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Access, modify, or delete data beyond what is necessary to demonstrate the vulnerability</li>
  <li>Perform denial-of-service attacks</li>
  <li>Conduct social engineering against organisation employees</li>
  <li>Disclose vulnerability details to third parties before the vendor is notified</li>
  <li>Use vulnerabilities for personal gain beyond legitimate bug bounty rewards</li>
</ul>

<h2>CVE Assignment</h2>
<p>For significant vulnerabilities, I request CVE IDs through appropriate CNAs (CVE Numbering Authorities) including MITRE and vendor-specific CNAs. All CVE advisories on this site are published post-patch.</p>

<h2>Legal Safe Harbour</h2>
<p>My research is conducted in good faith in compliance with applicable law including the Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (US), Computer Misuse Act 1990 (UK), and equivalent EU legislation. I expect organisations to recognise good-faith security research and not pursue legal action against researchers operating within this policy.</p>

<h2>Contact for Disclosure</h2>
<p>To report a vulnerability to me (e.g., in my own tools or infrastructure), use the <a href="/contact/">contact page</a> and encrypt your message with my <a href="/pgp/">PGP key</a>.</p>
